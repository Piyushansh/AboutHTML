Ayushi Panwar

Every time I look at her, I’m reminded of how lucky I am to have someone so beautiful, inside and out, in my life.

Her smile lights up my world like the sun on a perfect morning,
and her laughter is the sweetest music to my ears.

She’s not just my girlfriend; she’s my best friend, my confidante, and the most amazing person I’ve ever known.

With her by my side, even the simplest moments feel magical, and every day feels like a new adventure filled with love and happiness.
